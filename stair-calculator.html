About Stair Calculations

This calculator visualizes the relationship between stair dimensions, balustrades, and rails. The calculations follow standard building code requirements and best practices for stair construction.

Key relationships:

  • Rake Angle: Calculated as arctan(Rise/Run)
  • Net Baluster Height: Rail height minus rail thickness
  • Baluster Setbacks: Evenly distributed along the tread
  • Net Baluster Heights: Increase with distance from nosing due to the rake angle

To print: Use Ctrl+P (Windows) or Cmd+P (Mac) and select "Landscape" orientation for best results.
